About PianoMaster

PianoMaster is the world's most advanced interactive piano platform. We combine cutting-edge web technology with professional music pedagogy to help anyone learn the piano from the comfort of their home.

Our platform features a full 88-key professional piano engine powered by Tone.js, a library of 100+ popular songs, and structured lessons that make learning fun and effective.
